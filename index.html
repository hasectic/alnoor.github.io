About Us

AL-NOOR EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY (Nazra) light of "faith" for better "education"

Features :- weekly class conducted by our team
monthly test series conducted by our team
guess book for all subject provided by our team
mid month test conducted by our team
yearly examination conducted by our team
Note-:All facilities provided without any cost means *free

Al-Noor Educational society-Nazra (ANESN) is a charitable trust.non-profit making social service organization, established to promote education amongst educationally backward sections of the society. It mission is to make available good quality education to each & every student..This trust provides educational help to all sections of society. Today, GET is engaged in imparting education in madhubani..It also caters to Std. IX and Std. X students in various Government schools and the academic results of those children have been excellent. It also works with children in tribal areas.
